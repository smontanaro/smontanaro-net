Prestige (1985,1986,1989)

Started in 1985 the Prestige was made with Tange's highest grade tubing "Tange Prestige" in 1989 The Montogomery Securities/Avenir Team used the Prestige as their Team bikes. the Frame geometry is setup for Criterium

1985 Specs
Frame Construction Full Tange Prestige Double butted Chrome Moly, All investment cast lugs
Fork Tange Prestige, Fluted Steering tube, Investment Cast Crown
Brake Shimano New Dura-Ace Sidepull, Hooded New Dura-Ace Levers
Chain DID Dai Hard Bevelled plates Chrome Plated
Crankset Shimano New Dura-Ace Dark Anodized. 52/42t Anatomic Sizing
Derailleurs Shimano New Dura-Ace dark anodized, S.I.S system, Brazed on Levers
Freewheel Shimano New Dura-Ace. Thread on type 13-21 t 6-speed
Handlebar Nitto 175 Aluminum Alloy. Pelten padded tape. Anatomic Sizing
Headset Shimano New Dura-Ace Alloy Sealed System.
Hubs Shimano New Dura-Ace 32h Sealed system
Pedals Shimano New Dura-Ace aero with integrated toe clip/strap
Rims Mavic GP-4 Hard anodized 32 hole for tubular tires
Seat Selle Italia Turbo Genuine Suede Calfskin
Seatpost Shimano Dura-Ace EX, 27.4mm
Spokes Asahi Stainless Steel 14-15-14 Ga
Stem Nitto NTC-A, Forged Aluminum Alloy, Anatomic Sizing
Tires Vittoria Nuovo Pro 260gm. Cotton Casing tubular
Frame Sizes 50, 52, 54, 56, 58, 60, 62, 64cm
Colors Anthracite with Silver Two Tone seat/head tubes Protective Clearcoat
