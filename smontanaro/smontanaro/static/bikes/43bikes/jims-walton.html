This frame was built for my buddy Jim M. by Mike Walton of Tucson. It was originally light blue and white with Dura-Ace 7 speed parts.
It's the twin of my race bike - my '87 Walton - with identical dimensions and the same Columbus SL/SP tubing. This one survived many seasons of racing, won races, and had a nasty crash in the years in the years up to when I bought the frame and began its restoration (restortion?) in 2001.
